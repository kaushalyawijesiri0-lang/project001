# 🏠 Smart Boarding Finder – University Student Accommodation Platform

## 📌 Overview

Smart Boarding Finder is a web-based platform designed to help university students easily find and book boarding places near their campus. This system specifically addresses a common issue faced by students: **paying for unused boarding rooms due to early booking requirements**.

This idea is based on real-life experiences of students at the **University of Moratuwa**, where many students pay rent months before their academic activities begin.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Students face several challenges when searching for boarding places:

* Need to book rooms **months in advance**
* Paying rent for **unused periods (3–6 months)**
* Lack of visibility of **future vacancies**
* No centralized, trusted platform
* Risk of **fake or low-quality boarding places**

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

This platform introduces a **smart boarding management system** that allows students to:

* Find verified boarding places
* Book both **current and upcoming vacancies**
* Pay only **advance instead of full rent**
* Use a **trusted and user-friendly system**

---

## 🌟 Key Feature: Upcoming Vacancy Booking

Unlike existing systems, this platform allows boarding owners to post **future vacancies**.

### 🔍 Example:

* A 3rd-year student leaves for an internship in May
* A 4th-year student moves to university hostel

👉 These rooms can be listed as **"upcoming vacancies"**

### ✅ Benefits:

* Students can **plan ahead**
* No need to pay for unused months
* Boarding owners avoid empty rooms

---

## 🏗️ System Features

### 🏠 Boarding Listings

Each boarding place includes:

* Photos
* Monthly rent (with/without electricity)
* Advance payment
* Room type (single/shared)
* Gender preference
* Distance to university
* Bed type (thattu adan, single, double)
* Facilities:

  * Cooking
  * Study area
  * Parking
  * Washroom type
  * Hot water
  * Washing machine

---

### 🔍 Smart Search & Filters

Users can filter boarding places by:

* Price range
* Distance to university
* Room type
* Facilities
* Gender
* **Number of available slots (important for groups)**

---

### 👥 Group Accommodation Support

* Filter rooms by **remaining vacancies (2, 3, 4, 6, etc.)**
* Ideal for students coming from distant areas as groups

---

### 📅 Booking System

* Book **current or upcoming vacancies**
* Pay **advance through payment gateway**
* Platform ensures **trusted connection**

---

### 👤 Owner Registration

Boarding owners can:

* Register and manage their listings
* Add/update boarding details
* Upload photos
* Post current and future vacancies
* Quickly update availability

---

### 💳 Payment System

* Students pay **advance via platform**
* Owners pay **annual registration fee**
* Secure online payment integration

---

### ⭐ Review & Rating System

* Students can leave reviews
* Ratings help maintain quality and trust
* Prevents fake listings

---

### 🔄 Real-Time Updates

* Owners can update availability instantly
* Prevents double booking and outdated data

---

### 📢 Promotion Support

* Owners can promote listings
* Share via social media (e.g., Facebook)

---

## 💼 Business Model

* 🧾 Annual fee from boarding owners
* 💰 Commission from booking advances
* 📢 Paid promotions for listings

---

## 🎯 Target Users

* University students (especially first-year)

* Boarding house owners
